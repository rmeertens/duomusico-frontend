Frequently asked questions

How do you score songs?

Using an algorithm called TF-IDF. I first determined for each word in every lyric how "important" that word is for language understanding. If a word occurs very often (like the word: "me"), then it's not really interesting to server somebody a lyric with only that word a lot of times if he already knows more words

Do songs keep changing over time?

Yes. Each time you learn more words the recommendations change. Make sure you check back often to get the latest recommendations for your level of knowledge!

Why doesn't this work for language X?

It's pretty difficult to find lists with lyrics in all languages. I added a lot of songs I know, but I don't know songs in all languages. If you happen to have a list of cool songs in a language I'm missing, please send it to me!

Do you make any money thanks to this site?

No, this site still standing after a research project. As it's pretty cool I never took it down. I don't have any advertisements or other ways of monetizing this site and I don't plan on that. In fact, I have to pay to keep it in the air.
